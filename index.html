Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Void Codex is a free, unofficial fan tool for Warframe. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. This app runs entirely in your browser.

Data We Don't Collect

We do not collect your name, email, IP address, location, device identifiers, or any other personal data. There are no accounts, no sign-ups, and no tracking of any kind.

Local Storage

Your tracked relics are saved using your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

Void Codex fetches Warframe drop table data from drops.warframestat.us and relic information from wiki.warframe.com. These are read-only requests — no personal data is sent to these services.

Cookies & Ads

Void Codex uses no cookies and displays no advertisements.
